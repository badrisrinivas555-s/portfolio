The problem it solves

Sales leaders and operations managers lose hours every week to inbox overload — reading emails, extracting action items, trying to remember what came in. This workflow removes that entirely. Every email is processed by AI, the key information is pulled out, and it's saved to Notion automatically. Your inbox becomes a structured knowledge base.

The projects above didn't come from a course or a YouTube tutorial. They came from knowing — firsthand — what breaks in a sales operation and what it costs.
