Project 01 · Zapier · Flagship
Zapier ✦ AI Step Live
AI-Powered Interior
Quotation Generator
A customer fills a form with their property type, materials, finishes and add-ons. Zapier pulls live pricing data from a Google Sheet, passes it to AI, which generates two full quotes — Budget and Standard. A branded Google Doc is created instantly, emailed to the customer, and the sales team is notified on Slack — all without a single manual step.
Google Forms Google Sheets AI by Zapier Google Docs Gmail Slack
The problem it solves

In interior design sales, generating a quotation manually takes hours — pricing lookup, document formatting, email drafting, team coordination. High-intent customers wait too long and go cold. This workflow compresses that entire process into seconds. The customer gets a professional, itemised quote before the sales rep even picks up the phone.

What makes this advanced
✦ Dynamic pricing pulled live from Google Sheets
✦ AI generates Budget + Standard quote versions
✦ Google Doc created automatically per customer
✦ Branded email sent + Slack alert fired instantly

Project 02 · Python · HuggingFace · Flagship
HuggingFace Python ✦ AI Step Live
InterioAssist AI —
Dual-Domain AI Assistant
A production-ready AI assistant built with Python, Groq's Llama-3.1 model, and a custom Gradio UI — deployed live on HuggingFace Spaces. It handles two expert domains in one interface: interior design guidance (layouts, materials, Vastu, budgets) and sales performance analytics (funnel analysis, conversion rates, team improvement). This is not a tutorial project. It is a fully deployed, publicly accessible AI product.
Python Groq API Llama-3.1 Gradio UI HuggingFace Spaces Prompt Engineering
🚀 View Live Demo
The problem it solves

Interior design sales teams spend hours answering repetitive customer queries — material costs, room layout ideas, Vastu rules, budget tiers. At the same time, sales managers lack a fast way to analyse team performance and conversion data. This assistant handles both. One AI, two expert domains, deployed as a live product anyone can use.

What makes this advanced
✦ Custom system prompt — dual-domain expert logic engineered from scratch
✦ Groq API integrated — fast, low-latency Llama-3.1 inference
✦ Deployed live on HuggingFace — public URL, auto-rebuild on commit
✦ Full chat memory — history passed correctly to model each turn
✦ Indian home context — Vastu rules, local material names, INR pricing

Project 04 · Make.com · Enterprise
Make.com Live
Automotive Lead
Distribution System
In a busy dealership, every minute a lead sits unassigned is a lost sale. This workflow reads the vehicle type from a new lead entry and instantly routes it to the right specialist team on Slack — while copying the branch manager on every single lead through a private channel, without the sales floor knowing.
The problem it solves

Walk-in leads were being manually assigned — wrong vehicle type reaching the wrong consultant, delays in response, no visibility for management. This automation removes all of that. Lead comes in, route fires, right team gets it, manager sees it — in seconds.

Project 05 · Make.com
Make.com Live
Smart Lead Routing
to CRM & Slack
Interior design leads from two experience centers were landing in a single shared inbox — wrong consultant, delayed responses, no CRM record. This workflow reads the city and budget from every new lead, routes it to the correct team channel, and auto-creates the contact in HubSpot with zero manual input.
The problem it solves

In a multi-branch interior design operation, leads from two experience centers were being manually forwarded. Budget-based team assignment (Premium vs Standard) was guesswork. This router automates both decisions simultaneously — team gets the lead, CRM gets the contact, in one flow.
